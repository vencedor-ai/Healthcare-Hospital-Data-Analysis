Problem Statement
Healthcare Data Analysis Power BI project
A Nigerian-Based Healthcare organization wants us to create a sales dashboard showing information of YTD Waitlist and generate
insights  for below scenarios:

1.Create a KPI Banner showing YTD Patient, PYTD Patient, YTD Inpatient,YTD Outpatient, YTD Day Case
2.Find YOY growth for each KPI above.
3.Find PYTD Day Case, PYTD Inpatient and PYTD Outpatient
4.Find the YTD Patient,PYTD Patient,YOY Patient growth for different Patient Age category using a table chart.
5.Find Total Waitlist patient case type
6.Find the Mean, Median for different patient case_type using table chart


Deliverables
1. Prepare a comprehensive report in a PowerPoint highlighting the Key findings.
2. Explain the business impact of your findings and how they can inform decision-making
3. Propose at least two strategic actions based on your analysis that could help improve business outcomes.
